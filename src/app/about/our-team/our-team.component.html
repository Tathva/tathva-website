Our Team

A top class education requires world class educators. At Tathva we are proud to have a teaching body comprising of experienced, qualified and professional teachers from a number of countries, including: Canada, the UK, Hong Kong, USA, Philippines, Australia, Japan, China and India. This allows our students access to a truly international experience, not only following the Cambridge curriculum, but also adapting techniques from the US Common Core, the UK National Curriculum and educational systems from countries around the world.

At Tathva we aspire to create culturally sensitive, environmentally responsible global citizens who are prepared and capable of becoming the leaders of tomorrow's generation.
