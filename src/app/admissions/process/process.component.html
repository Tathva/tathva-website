How To Apply

1. Review the Admissions Handbook
Download Admissions Handbook

Please download the Admissions Handbook to understand the complete Admission Process and school rules.

Please also read the appropriate sections of our website for information on class placement, fee structure and bus routes.

2. Send in Admissions Inquiry and Admission Application Form
Download Admission Application Form

Send an inquiry mail to admissions@tathva.ed.jp with a completed Admission Application Form.

The information provided will be verified internally and an appointment will be provided to you by Admissions Team.

3. School Visit and Assessment

On the day of visit, please bring a Photo identification card of yourself and the child.
Please bring required documents as mentioned below.

An overview about our school mission, philosophy, and curriculum will be given during your visit to school.

Completed Admission Application form and required documents are mandatory for an assessment to be conducted.
An application fee of ¥5,400 will charged for the assessment.
If an assessment is planned, the entire process will take 2-3 hours. The application fee is non-refundable.

The assessment will be conducted by the academic team. A prior appointment is mandatory for an assessment. An assessment can be planned on the same day or on a different day.

4. Respond to Admission Decision

Following the interview and in-school assessment, student documents will be verified by our admissions team. Provided that all documents have been submitted on time, the results of the in-school assessment will be sent to parents via email within 7 working days.

Acknowledgement of the acceptance by the parent within 5 business days followed by payment of the initial invoice will secure the admission.

If any information provided to the school during admission process, is found to be falsified, then the school reserves the right to cancel the admission and initiate separation process.

Admission Policy Information

English as a Second Language

Students with limited English ability will be accepted on a case-by-case decision, depending on the space-availability and prior educational experiences.

ESL students can be accepted with no knowledge of English up to Grade 5, provided they have a strong academic background and if the student performs well in our assessment test.

At least one of the parents should be able to speak and read English, as all school correspondences will be conducted in English only. If neither of the parents are able to speak English, they must bring their own interpreter each time. The school will not divulge any information to another parent if brought as an interpreter.

Special Needs

All students accepted to Tathva International School are expected to function in the regular classroom independently.

It is important to note that the ongoing enrollment at Tathva International School for students will be based upon the students’ ability to function within the regular program.

Tathva International School does not have the facilities or resources to accommodate children who have significant learning, emotional or physical disabilities. Parents of a child receiving any special services, such as learning disabled instruction, remedial support, speech/language therapy, occupational/physical therapy, behavioral support, or seeing any educational specialist outside of the regular classroom, must disclose the additional services that are being implemented with your child. Supporting documentation from a school psychologist, previous school, and/or pediatrician may be requested by administration to complete the admission process and/or continue at Tathva International School.
