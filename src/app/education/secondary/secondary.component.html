Secondary & IGCSE Education

Secondary instruction takes place in a separate building. Under the guidance of our high class Secondary teaching team grades 5 through IGCSE follow the Cambridge curriculum in a range of subjects, including but not limited to Maths, Science, Social Science, English as a First Language, Japanese, Hindi, Enterprise and ICT.

We strive to teach our students not only the value of excellent academic achievement, but emphasise the value of community, achievements in other aspects of life, and the importance of living well. We also foster the students’ creativity and ingenuity through different activities (e.g. field trips), projects (e.g. studying ecosystems through keeping an aquarium and a garden), and after school clubs.

Every year a number of events such as Annual Day, the PTA-led Autumn Fair, and Sports Day contribute to the students’ education and unforgettable school experience. Students are also given the opportunity to participate in spelling bees and speech and drama competitions, as well as external tests such as International Olympiad.

Tathva International is a university preparatory school which aims to lay the foundations for eminence in the future lives of our students.
